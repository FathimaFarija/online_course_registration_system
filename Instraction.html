Online Student Registration Website Instructions

Overview

This website has two main sections:
1. Student Page
2. Registration Page

How to apply the course?

1. Student Registration:

- To register, the student clicks on the registration link which redirects them to the Registration Page.
- The student fills in the necessary information to create a username and password.

2. Login and Profile Setup:

- After successful registration, the student logs in using their newly created username and password.
- Upon logging in, the student is taken to their profile page where they can browse courses, view course details, and see the cost of each course.

3. Browse and select the course:

- The student visits the Student Page.
- On this page, the student can browse available courses and view detailed information about each course, including cost details.

4. Course Registration:

- The student selects the desired courses and registers for them.
- Ather that system will provides the status of the student, indicating whether they are "selected", "rejected", or "waiting".

5. Payment and Confirmation:

- If the student is selected, they need to pay the registration fees.
- The student can make the payment through various methods, including Visa card.
- After the payment is made, the student must upload the deposit slip or screenshot for authentication.

6. Verification and Registration Number:

- After verifying the payment and other provided information, the system issues a registration number to the student.
-After submit the diposit , this system ask Confirmation for registration. you want to confirm, then only it will show the confirmation message

7. About Us Page:

- The student visits the About Us Page to learn more about the university.
- The page provides detailed information about the university, its mission, vision, history, and other relevant details.
- Students can leave comments on the page to share their thoughts or ask questions.
